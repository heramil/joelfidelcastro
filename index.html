Obituary

Joel Fidel Castro, beloved husband, father, brother, uncle, and friend, passed away on March 13, 2024, surrounded by his family and loved ones. He was born on April 24, 1947 in the Philippines to Balbino and Julita Castro. He grew up in Pasig City with five brothers and three sisters.

Joel's life was a testament to compassion, integrity, and unwavering faith. He was the first in his family to graduate college with a Bachelor of Science in Commerce Accounting. He worked as a professional accountant in the Philippines, then in New York and California.

Yet, it was Joel's devotion to his faith and family that truly defined him. A loving husband to Chona Katigbak Castro and a nurturing father to Jonathan and Jocelyn, he led his household with humility and grace, instilling in them the values of kindness, empathy, and service to others.

Joel's unwavering faith in God was the cornerstone of his life. He found solace and strength in his spiritual journey, guiding him through life's joys and challenges with steadfast resolve. He was baptized and married at Central Filipino Seventh Day Adventist Church and served as a director for Adventurers, Pathfinders, and Vacation Bible School and as the church treasurer. His genuine kindness and generous spirit touched the lives of many. His legacy of love, faith, and service will forever inspire us to live our lives with purpose and compassion.

A memorial service to celebrate Joel's life will be on March 30, 2024 at Central Filipino Church. Followed by a viewing on April 1, 2024 at Forest Lawn Covina and the funeral on April 5, 2024. Donations sent to the family will go towards the funeral expenses. Joel will be deeply missed but never forgotten.
